# New Employee Onboarding Guide

## Welcome to TechFlow!

Congratulations on joining our team! This guide will help you get started during your first weeks at TechFlow.

## Your First Day

### Morning Checklist
1. Check in with HR to complete paperwork
2. Collect your laptop and equipment from IT
3. Meet your manager for orientation
4. Get your badge and building access
5. Set up your desk and workstation

### Account Setup
You'll receive emails with setup instructions for:
- Company email (Gmail)
- Slack workspace
- TechFlow platform (of course!)
- GitHub organization access
- 1Password team vault
- Zoom account

All passwords should be stored in 1Password. Never share passwords via email or Slack.

## Your First Week

### Day 1-2: Orientation
- Company overview presentation with the People team
- Product demo and platform walkthrough
- Meet your team members
- Review team goals and current projects

### Day 3-4: Technical Setup
- Development environment setup (follow the Dev Setup Guide)
- Code review process training
- CI/CD pipeline overview
- Access to development, staging, and production environments

### Day 5: Team Integration
- Shadow a team member
- Attend team standup
- Review your 30-60-90 day plan with your manager
- Start your first onboarding task

## Key Resources

### Documentation
- **Confluence**: Company wiki and documentation
- **Notion**: Team-specific notes and meeting records
- **GitHub Wiki**: Technical documentation and architecture docs

### Communication
- **Slack**: Primary communication tool
  - #general: Company-wide announcements
  - #engineering: Technical discussions
  - #random: Fun and casual conversations
  - Your team channel will be shared by your manager
- **Email**: For external communication and formal requests

### Meetings
- **Daily Standup**: 9:30 AM, 15 minutes
- **Weekly Team Sync**: Wednesdays at 2 PM
- **Sprint Planning**: Every other Monday
- **All Hands**: Monthly, first Friday

## Support & Questions

### Who to Contact
- **IT Issues**: #it-help on Slack or it@techflow.io
- **HR Questions**: hr@techflow.io or book time with People team
- **Technical Questions**: Your tech lead or #engineering-help
- **Expense/Finance**: finance@techflow.io

### Your Onboarding Buddy
You'll be assigned an onboarding buddy who:
- Will check in with you daily during your first week
- Can answer questions about company culture
- Will help you navigate internal tools and processes
- Is available for coffee chats and casual questions

## 30-60-90 Day Expectations

### First 30 Days
- Complete all onboarding training
- Understand team processes and workflows
- Ship your first small contribution
- Build relationships with immediate team members

### Days 31-60
- Take ownership of a small feature or project
- Participate actively in code reviews
- Contribute to technical discussions
- Identify areas for improvement

### Days 61-90
- Lead a small project or initiative
- Mentor newer team members
- Demonstrate independence in daily work
- Complete probation review with manager

## Company Culture

### Our Values
1. **Customer Obsession**: Every decision starts with the customer
2. **Bias for Action**: Move fast, learn faster
3. **Radical Transparency**: Share information openly
4. **Continuous Improvement**: Always be learning

### Social Events
- Monthly team happy hours
- Quarterly all-hands celebrations
- Annual company retreat
- Interest-based clubs (running, gaming, book club)
