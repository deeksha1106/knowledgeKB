# Company Policies & Guidelines

## Remote Work Policy

### Eligibility
All full-time employees who have completed their probation period (90 days) are eligible for remote work arrangements. Remote work is a privilege, not a right, and may be modified based on business needs.

### Working Hours
- Core hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM in your local timezone
- You must be available for meetings and collaboration during core hours
- Flexible hours outside of core hours are permitted with manager approval

### Equipment & Setup
- The company provides a laptop and monitor for all remote workers
- A $500 home office stipend is available annually for ergonomic equipment
- Reliable internet connection (minimum 50 Mbps) is required
- VPN must be used for all company network access

### Communication Expectations
- Respond to Slack messages within 2 hours during work hours
- Keep your calendar updated with availability
- Enable video for all team meetings unless bandwidth issues occur
- Weekly 1:1 meetings with your manager are mandatory

## Paid Time Off (PTO) Policy

### Annual Allocation
- 0-2 years of service: 15 days PTO
- 2-5 years of service: 20 days PTO
- 5+ years of service: 25 days PTO

### Requesting Time Off
- Submit PTO requests at least 2 weeks in advance
- Emergency time off should be communicated to your manager immediately
- PTO must be approved by your direct manager
- Unused PTO does not carry over to the next year (use it or lose it)

### Holidays
The company observes 10 paid holidays per year:
- New Year's Day
- Martin Luther King Jr. Day
- Presidents' Day
- Memorial Day
- Independence Day
- Labor Day
- Thanksgiving (2 days)
- Christmas Eve
- Christmas Day

## Expense Reimbursement

### Eligible Expenses
- Business travel (flights, hotels, transportation)
- Client entertainment (with prior approval)
- Professional development and training
- Home office equipment (within annual budget)

### Submission Process
1. Submit expenses through ExpenseBot within 30 days of incurrence
2. Include itemized receipts for all expenses over $25
3. Categorize expenses correctly
4. Manager approval required for expenses over $500
5. Reimbursement processed within 10 business days

## Code of Conduct

### Professional Behavior
- Treat all colleagues with respect and dignity
- Maintain confidentiality of company and client information
- Report conflicts of interest immediately
- Dress appropriately for video calls and in-person meetings

### Anti-Harassment Policy
We have zero tolerance for harassment of any kind. Report any incidents to HR immediately through our confidential reporting system.
